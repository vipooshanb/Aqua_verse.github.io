Coral Reefs

Coral reefs are among the most diverse ecosystems on Earth, supporting approximately 25% of all marine species despite covering less than 1% of the ocean floor.

  • Provide habitat for marine life
  • Protect coastlines from storms
  • Support fishing and tourism industries
  • Act as natural water filters

Coastal Guardians

Mangroves

Mangrove forests are coastal ecosystems that provide critical habitat for numerous species and serve as natural barriers against storms and erosion.

  • Nursery grounds for fish
  • Carbon sequestration
  • Filter pollutants from water
  • Stabilize shorelines
